A Little About Me

I'm an environmental engineer, handicrafter and donut enthusiast. After stints in academia and the consulting world, I'm now exploring code as a new outlet for creativity and practical problem solving.

I recently graduated from General Assembly's 12-week Web Development Immersive Program, a full-time course that taught the fundamentals of Ruby, Rails, Javascript, jQuery, coffeescript, SQL, HTML5, CSS, and RSpec.
